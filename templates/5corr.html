Underlying Conditions and Symptoms Correlation










Considerations

To observe any correlation between pre-existing conditions and symptoms, we used matplotlib and seaborn to create a correlation matrix for each feature. Higher correlation is noted in patient populations with underlying combinations of COPD/lung disease and diabetes or COPD/lung disease and chronic kidney disease. Within the hypertension feature, there was a higher association with shortness of breath followed by fatigue. Fever and cough symptoms were correlated as well as cough and diarrhea.
