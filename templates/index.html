Our Data

Intent: Our project is to use machine learning to identify patient populations at risk for COVID 19 mortality.

Data used for the project was based on a compilation of international research publications created by ***. Each row represents a population of patients with a rich amount of medical information: such as percentage breakdown of gender, ethnicity, past travel and medical history, symptoms, treatment plan and mean population values for age, lab results (CBC, WBC, liver enzymes) to list a few.

We focused on age, gender, medical history (pre-existing conditions) and symptoms. The dataset was filtered to include populations with adequately reported data and the dataset size was augmented to allow for application of machine learning. All pertaining data to our selected population were tested COVID 19 positive. To apply logistic regression model in determining populations at low or high covid19 mortality risk, the rate of mortality was converted to binary values using the mean of the column as a sorting factor.
